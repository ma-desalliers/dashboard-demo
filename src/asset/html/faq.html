FAQs About Electric Trailer Brakes  

How do electric trailer brakes work?

Electric trailer brakes use an electric current to activate brake magnets, which then engage the drum and brake shoes to slow the trailer.

Are electric brakes better than hydraulic brakes?

Electric brakes are often preferred for their adjustability and ease of installation. However, the choice depends on your towing needs and trailer type.

Can I install electric trailer brakes myself?

Yes, Frameco’s kits are designed for straightforward installation, with pre-wired systems, pre-greased bearings, and detailed instructions.

Do I need a brake controller?

Yes, a brake controller is essential for operating electric brakes and adjusting their intensity to match your towing conditions.

Are electric trailer brakes compatible with all trailers?

Frameco’s electric brakes are versatile and fit most trailers, especially those equipped with Dexter axles and drum setups.
